IBM IBM is a company

IBM is a company that is known for its technology and innovation. It was founded in 1911 and has grown to become one of the largest and most successful companies in the world. IBM is involved in a wide range of industries, including computer hardware, software, and services. The company is known for its research and development efforts, and has been responsible for many technological breakthroughs over the years. IBM is also a major player in the business world, providing a wide range of products and services to companies of all sizes.
